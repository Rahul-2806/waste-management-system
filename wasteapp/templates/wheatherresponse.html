Response Timeline

Pre-Weather Event (24-48 Hours Before)

  • Monitor weather forecasts
  • Prepare alternative waste collection routes
  • Check and maintain drainage systems

During Weather Event

  • Activate emergency response team
  • Implement adaptive routing
  • Continuous communication with field staff

Post-Weather Event

  • Assess infrastructure damage
  • Clear backlogged waste collection routes
  • Conduct equipment and vehicle maintenance

Key Response Protocols

Route Adaptation

Prioritize essential waste collection areas. Use real-time GPS tracking to adjust routes dynamically.

Safety Measures

Ensure staff safety with protective gear and emergency communication devices.

Equipment Protection

Waterproof and secure critical waste management equipment.

Contingency Scheduling

Create backup shifts and extended operation hours if needed.
